Tasks

  • What is the name of your app? Live ChatRoom
  • What collaborative activity can people do together using your app? Join and Create their own topic of chat upon a movie or a sports game.
  • What is the ideal number of concurrent users (must be at least 10 but no more than 40)?10~40
  • What skills do people need in order to do this activity? Typing
  • Is this activity for fun or for something practical? For recreation and time killing
  • How can a person see what everyone else is doing? Once login, he will be able to see the ongoing topics and those who joined in
  • How can a person see the most recent result of everyone's collaborative effort? It will be displayed in the time order in the chat boxes
  • What can an admin see (i.e., God's mode)? The list of all the ongoing topics, participants in this topics and chat history.
